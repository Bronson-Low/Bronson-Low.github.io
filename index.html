What I Do

I specialize my work around natural light outdoors, photographing friends, family, or pets. I enjoy looking for beautiful lighting to make my subjects look awesome. My photo sessions typically last 15-30 minutes, commonly in golden hour or during other amazing lighting conditions.

About Me

Hello, I'm Bronson Low, a passionate photographer local to Logan, Utah. I enjoy playing piano, learning more about aviation, and delving deep into technical photography jargon. My strong curiosity and love of light led me to become a photographer. Though I'm still learning, I find true joy every time I pick up my camera. Right now I'm currently building my portfolio, so if you're open to working with someone still learning but genuinely passionate, I'd love to photograph you.
